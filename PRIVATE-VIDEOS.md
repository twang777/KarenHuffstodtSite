## Recovery Options

### 1. Contact Video Uploaders
- Try to identify and contact uploaders through YouTube channel info
- Request they make videos public/unlisted or provide direct copies
- Request viewer access if they're willing to share credentials

### 2. Contact Venues/Opera Companies
- Reach out to the opera houses, festivals, or production companies
- They may have official archives or contact info for uploaders
- They may have rights to the recordings

### 3. Check Internet Archive
- Search https://archive.org/web/ using the full YouTube URLs
- Success rate is low but worth checking

### 4. Search for Re-uploads
- Search YouTube with specific performance details (opera, venue, year, cast)
- Other attendees may have uploaded their own recordings
- Google search with performance details

### 5. Check for Alternative Sources
- Venue websites may host performance videos
- Opera company archives
- Professional video services or databases
